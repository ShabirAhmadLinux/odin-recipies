Short description :

Rogan Josh is a signature dish of Kashmiri cuisine, known for its deep red color, rich flavor, and aromatic spices. It typically consists of tender lamb or goat meat cooked in a mixture of yogurt, garlic, and various spices such as cloves, bay leaves, cardamom, and cinnamon. The vibrant red hue of the dish comes from Kashmiri red chilies, which are milder in heat but rich in color, giving Rogan Josh its distinctive appearance without overwhelming spiciness. This dish is often enjoyed with steamed rice or Indian bread, making it a hearty and comforting meal.

The preparation of Rogan Josh is a labor of love, where the meat is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the flavors to meld and the meat to become incredibly tender. The use of traditional Kashmiri spices and ingredients gives this dish its unique and authentic taste, making it a true reflection of the region's culinary heritage. Whether enjoyed as part of a festive meal or a comforting family dinner, Rogan Josh is a timeless classic that continues to win hearts with its rich and aromatic profile.
